## 🚀 How to Use:

### **1. Create Professional Templates**
1. **Go to Templates** → Click "Visual Editor" tab
2. **Choose Quick Template** → Click any template button
3. **Customize colors** → Change background, text, button colors
4. **Upload images** → Replace sample images with your own
5. **Edit text** → Click text elements to edit content
6. **Save template** → Use in email campaigns

### **2. Upload Custom Images**
1. **Add Image** → Click "Add Image" button
2. **Select element** → Click the image on canvas
3. **Upload new** → In properties panel, click "Choose Image"
4. **Select file** → Choose JPG, PNG, or GIF
5. **Instant update** → Image appears on canvas

### **3. Change Background**
1. **Color picker** → Left sidebar, bottom
2. **Choose color** → Click any color
3. **Live preview** → Canvas updates immediately
4. **Professional look** → Match your brand colors
